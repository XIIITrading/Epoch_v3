## System Identity

Method is a **volume-based supply and demand zone trading methodology** for US equity intraday markets. The core thesis: price reacts predictably at high-volume price levels (HVN POCs) where institutional participation concentrates. The system identifies these levels pre-market, monitors real-time indicator conditions during the session, and validates edges statistically through backtesting.

**Key principle**: The system surfaces opportunities. Silva decides which to take.

## Zone Construction

### Volume Profile Foundation

Zones are built from **High Volume Node (HVN) Points of Control** — price levels with the highest traded volume within a defined epoch period. Volume profiles use $0.01-granularity resolution.

- **Top 10 HVN POCs** are extracted per ticker per day
- Each POC becomes the anchor for a zone

### Zone Boundaries

```
zone_high = hvn_poc + (M15_ATR / 2)
zone_low  = hvn_poc - (M15_ATR / 2)
```

Zone width is ATR-adaptive — wider zones in volatile instruments, narrower in calm ones.

### Confluence Scoring (L1-L5)

Each zone is scored by how many independent technical levels overlap with it. The system checks ~60+ levels per ticker including:
- Monthly/Weekly/Daily OHLC (prior period highs, lows, opens, closes)
- Camarilla pivots (S3/S4/S6, R3/R4/R6)
- Options open interest levels (op_01 through op_10)
- Prior day volume profile levels (PDV POC/VAH/VAL)
- Market structure levels (D1/H4/H1/M15 strong/weak levels)

**Bucket-max scoring**: Each confluence type contributes its MAX weight only once — no artificial score inflation from stacking multiple levels of the same type.

**L-Rank Thresholds** (total confluence score):

| Rank | Score | Meaning |
|------|-------|---------|
| L1 | 0-2 | Minimal confluence |
| L2 | 3-5 | Low confluence |
| L3 | 6-8 | Moderate confluence |
| L4 | 9-11 | Strong confluence |
| L5 | 12+ | Very strong confluence |

**Tier Classification**:

| Tier | Ranks | Quality |
|------|-------|---------|
| T1 | L1, L2 | Lower quality |
| T2 | L3 | Medium quality |
| T3 | L4, L5 | High quality |

### Zone Filtering

1. **Proximity filter**: Zones beyond 2x D1 ATR from current price are excluded
2. **Overlap elimination**: When zones overlap, highest confluence score wins
3. **Minimum floor**: Zones below L3 are filtered out (`MIN_ZONE_SCORE`)
4. **Maximum per ticker**: 10 zones retained

### Direction Assignment

Zone direction (Bull/Bear) is derived from a **weighted multi-timeframe composite score**:

| Timeframe | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| D1 | 1.5 |
| H4 | 1.5 |
| H1 | 1.0 |
| M15 | 0.5 |

Composite score determines whether zone is a support (Bull) or resistance (Bear) level.

### Setup Generation

Each filtered zone generates a setup with:
- **Direction**: LONG (Bull zones) / SHORT (Bear zones)
- **Zone Type**: PRIMARY (direct POC touch) / SECONDARY (target zone)
- **Target**: Nearest opposing zone → R:R ratio calculated
- **Target cascade**: 3R first, then 4R if no structural target found

## Five Core Indicators

These indicators are calculated per M1 bar during the live session and stored in `m1_indicator_bars` tables.

### 1. Candle Range %

**Formula**: `(high - low) / close * 100`

**Purpose**: Measures bar volatility. Extremely small candles indicate absorption zones where momentum stalls.

| Classification | Threshold | Trading Implication |
|---------------|-----------|-------------------|
| ABSORPTION | < 0.12% | **SKIP TRADE** — validated edge: -17pp win rate effect |
| LOW | 0.12% - 0.15% | Caution — low momentum |
| NORMAL | 0.15% - 0.20% | Has momentum — acceptable |
| HIGH | >= 0.20% | Strong signal — momentum confirmed |

**Critical**: The 0.12% absorption threshold is a **hard validated edge**. Trades with candle range < 0.12% have 33% win rate (vs 47% baseline).

### 2. Volume Delta

**Formula** (Bar Position Method):
```
bar_position = (close - low) / (high - low)
delta_multiplier = (2 * bar_position) - 1
bar_delta = volume * delta_multiplier
rolling_delta = sum(bar_delta[-5:])  # 5-bar rolling sum
```

**Interpretation**:
- Close at high → +1.0 multiplier (all buying pressure)
- Close at low → -1.0 multiplier (all selling pressure)
- Doji (zero range) → 0.5 neutral
- **Volume Delta Paradox**: SHORT trades win MORE when delta is POSITIVE (+13pp validated edge) — selling into rallies (contrarian positioning)

### 3. Volume Rate of Change (ROC)

**Formula**:
```
baseline_avg = mean(volume[-21:-1])  # 20-bar trailing average, exclude current
roc = ((current_volume - baseline_avg) / baseline_avg) * 100
```

**Output**: Percentage relative to recent average

| Level | Threshold | Meaning |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| Average | ~0% | Normal volume |
| Elevated | >= 30% | Momentum confirmation |
| High | >= 50% | Strong momentum |

### 4. SMA Configuration (SMA9 vs SMA21)

**Periods**: SMA9 (fast) and SMA21 (slow)

**Configuration Labels**:
- **BULL**: SMA9 > SMA21
- **BEAR**: SMA9 < SMA21
- **FLAT**: SMA9 = SMA21

**Momentum Detection** (10-bar lookback on spread ratio):
- **WIDENING**: ratio > 1.1 (trend strengthening)
- **NARROWING**: ratio < 0.9 (trend weakening)
- **FLAT**: ratio 0.9-1.1

**Wide Spread**: >= 0.15% of price indicates strong established trend

### 5. H1 Market Structure

**Method**: Anchor + Walk-Forward with BOS/CHoCH Detection (v3)

**Fractal Detection**: Williams 5-candle fractal (2 bars each side)
- Fractal High: bar_high > all bars within 2 on each side
- Fractal Low: bar_low < all bars within 2 on each side

**Structure Events**:
- **BOS (Break of Structure)**: Close above/below confirmed weak level → trend continuation
- **CHoCH (Change of Character)**: Close breaches strong level → trend reversal

**Walk-Forward Logic**:
- Anchors on oldest pair of consecutive same-type fractals
- Tiered lookback: (200, 350, 500) bars
- 30% retracement threshold anchors next swing level

**Output**:
- Direction: BULL (+1) / BEAR (-1) / NEUTRAL (0)
- Strong level: Invalidation price (trend reversal trigger)
- Weak level: Continuation target

**H1 is the highest-priority indicator**: +36pp validated edge when H1 Structure is NEUTRAL. Cached and refreshed once per hour during session.

## Entry Models

### Four Entry Types

| Model | Zone Type | Mechanism | Description |
|-------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| **EPCH1** | PRIMARY | Continuation | Price traverses through zone (opens below, closes above for LONG) |
| **EPCH2** | PRIMARY | Rejection | Price wicks into zone, closes back out (reversal) |
| **EPCH3** | SECONDARY | Continuation | Same as EPCH1 using secondary zone boundaries |
| **EPCH4** | SECONDARY | Rejection | Same as EPCH2 using secondary zone boundaries |

### Entry Detection (S15 bars)

- Entry timeframe: **S15 (15-second bars)** for precision entry detection
- Entry window: **09:30 - 15:30 ET**
- Price origin detection: Scans bar history in reverse to find most recent bar that closed OUTSIDE the zone
- Returns origin as 'BELOW', 'ABOVE', or None

### Exit Management (M5 bars)

Exit priority hierarchy (strict order):
1. **STOP_HIT**: M5 ATR(14) x 1.1 close-based stop (close must breach, not just wick)
2. **TARGET_HIT**: R5 target reached
3. **EOD**: 15:50 ET forced close (10 minutes before market close)

**Critical**: Stop is **close-based**, not price-based. Intrabar wicks through stop level do NOT trigger exit — the M5 bar must CLOSE beyond the stop.

## Canonical Win Condition

**Table**: `trades_m5_r_win_2` (production) / `trades_m5_r_win` (legacy)

**This is the sole source of truth for trade outcomes.** All modules evaluating wins/losses MUST reference this table's `is_winner` field. Never use `trades.is_winner`.

### Definition

```
Stop Distance = M5_ATR(14) × 1.1
1R = Stop Distance

WIN:  MFE (Maximum Favorable Excursion) >= 1R before stop hit
LOSS: Stop hit before reaching 1R
```

### R-Multiples

| Level | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1R | Breakeven (risk = reward) |
| 1.5R | Minimum positive expectancy threshold |
| 2R | Good trade |
| 3R+ | Highlight trade (Trade Reel candidate) |

**Baseline win rate**: 47% on 1.5R trades (this is the baseline for all edge detection).

## Edge Validation Framework

### Statistical Criteria (All Three Required)

| Criterion | Threshold | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| p-value | < 0.05 | Chi-squared statistical significance |
| Effect size | > 3.0 percentage points | Practical significance (win rate difference vs baseline) |
| Sample size | >= 30 (MEDIUM), >= 100 (HIGH) | Confidence level |

### Edge Health Monitoring

| Health | Condition |
|--------|-----------|
| **HEALTHY** | Within 5pp of stored value, same sign |
| **WEAKENING** | Same sign but >5pp lower than stored value |
| **DEGRADED** | Sign reversed OR p > 0.05 |
| **INCONCLUSIVE** | p > 0.20 |

### Current Validated Edges (as of 2026-03-21)

| Edge | Effect | Action | Mechanism |
|------|--------|--------|-----------|
| **H1 Structure NEUTRAL** | +36.0pp | TRADE when H1 = NEUTRAL | Strongest edge — structure uncertainty creates opportunity |
| **Absorption Zone Skip** | -17.0pp | SKIP when candle_range < 0.12% | Absorption zones kill momentum |
| **Volume Delta Paradox** | +13.0pp (range 5-21pp) | TRADE against order flow when misaligned | Contrarian: selling into rallies works |

## DOW AI Decision Framework

### Dual-Pass Analysis

1. **Pass 1 (Trader's Eye)**: Reviews raw M1 bars + indicators. Default is TRADE unless clear red flags.
2. **Pass 2 (System Decision)**: Same data + backtested edge context. Produces TRADE/NO_TRADE with HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW confidence.

### Decision Priority

1. **Check H1 Structure FIRST** (strongest edge, +36pp)
   - Aligned with trade direction: Strong TRADE
   - NEUTRAL: Acceptable trade
   - Opposed: Caution — only NO_TRADE if multiple factors unfavorable
2. **Support check**: Score >= 5, Range >= 0.12%, SMA spread aligned
3. **Baseline consideration**: Even "mediocre" setups have positive expectancy (>33% needed to break even on 1.5R)

### Contrarian Paradoxes

The SHORT scoring contains deliberate contrarian logic based on validated edges:
- **Volume Delta Paradox**: SHORT trades win MORE when volume delta is POSITIVE (selling into buying pressure yields +13pp edge)
- This is counterintuitive but statistically validated

## Critical Rules (Never Override)

1. **Sole source of truth** for outcomes: `trades_m5_r_win_2.is_winner` — never `trades.is_winner`
2. **Close-based stops**: M5 bar must CLOSE beyond stop, not just wick through
3. **Absorption skip is hard**: < 0.12% candle range = no trade (validated -17pp)
4. **H1 Structure is king**: +36pp edge — always check first
5. **30-trade minimum**: No statistical conclusions below 30 trades per group
6. **Bucket-max confluence**: No stacking same-type levels
7. **Silva decides**: System recommends, human executes
